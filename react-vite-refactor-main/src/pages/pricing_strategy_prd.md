# Kinetic Pricing Strategy (Pakistan Market)

## Market Context

- High price sensitivity; preference for low-cost monthly plans.
- Strong response to "Value Deals" and daily cost breakdowns.
- Trust is key: highlight trial and secure local payments (JazzCash/EasyPaisa).

## Pricing Tiers (PKR)

- **Weekly:** PKR 149 (Impulse/Trial)
- **Monthly:** PKR 499 (Anchor price, shown with 50% discount to PKR 249)
- **Yearly (Best Seller):** PKR 2,999 (~PKR 8/day) - Highlight 50% Savings.

## Feature Differentiation

- **Free:** Basic streak, 24h timer, manual tracking.
- **Premium:** AI Food Analysis, Smart Insights, Craving Prediction, Advanced Analytics.

## Conversion Triggers

- 3-Day Free Trial.
- Urgency: "Offer expires in 24 hours".
- Positioning: "Upgrade your discipline system".
